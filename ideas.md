# Demo E-Commerce Store — Design Brainstorm

## Context
A basic e-commerce website with Meta Pixel and CAPI integration. Needs product listings, cart, checkout, lead form, contact form, registration. The site is a testing ground for a pixel/CAPI analysis skill, so it should feel like a real store.

## Idea 1: "Scandinavian Craft" — Nordic Minimalism

**Design Movement**: Scandinavian design / Japandi fusion — clean, warm, functional.

**Core Principles**:
1. Warm neutrals over cold whites — cream, sand, warm grays
2. Generous whitespace as a luxury signal
3. Organic shapes and rounded corners to soften the grid
4. Photography-forward product presentation

**Color Philosophy**: Warm earth tones — sandy beige backgrounds, charcoal text, terracotta accents. The palette evokes handmade craft and natural materials, creating trust and warmth.

**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric masonry grid for products, full-bleed hero sections, off-center text alignment. Sidebar cart drawer instead of separate page.

**Signature Elements**:
- Soft drop shadows with warm hue shifts
- Thin hairline dividers with generous spacing
- Rounded pill-shaped buttons and tags

**Interaction Philosophy**: Gentle, spring-based animations. Hover states that subtly lift cards. Smooth page transitions.

**Animation**: Staggered fade-in on scroll for product grids. Cart icon bounces on add. Drawer slides in from right with spring physics.

**Typography System**: DM Serif Display for headings (elegant serif), DM Sans for body (clean geometric sans). Large heading sizes with tight letter-spacing.

## Idea 2: "Industrial Commerce" — Brutalist-Lite E-Commerce

**Design Movement**: Neo-brutalism meets industrial design — bold, high-contrast, unapologetic.

**Core Principles**:
1. Hard edges and thick borders over soft shadows
2. High contrast black/white with a single neon accent
3. Visible grid structure — the layout IS the design
4. Typography as the primary visual element

**Color Philosophy**: Near-black (#0a0a0a) backgrounds, bright white cards, electric lime (#c8ff00) as the sole accent. The stark contrast commands attention and feels modern/edgy.

**Layout Paradigm**: Strict column grid with visible gutters. Full-width product cards stacked vertically on mobile. Dense information display — no wasted space.

**Signature Elements**:
- Thick 2-3px borders on all interactive elements
- Uppercase monospace labels and category tags
- Offset card shadows (hard shadow, no blur, 4px offset)

**Interaction Philosophy**: Snappy, immediate feedback. No easing curves — instant state changes. Hover states that invert colors.

**Animation**: Hard cut transitions. Elements snap into place. Cart count pulses on update. No gradual fades.

**Typography System**: Space Grotesk for headings (geometric, slightly quirky), JetBrains Mono for labels/prices, Inter for body text. Heavy use of uppercase and letter-spacing.

## Idea 3: "Elevated Essentials" — Modern Editorial Commerce

**Design Movement**: Editorial design meets luxury e-commerce — magazine-like layouts with commercial functionality.

**Core Principles**:
1. Editorial grid with mixed column widths — 2/3 + 1/3 splits
2. Restrained color with strategic pops of deep teal
3. Layered depth through overlapping elements and z-index play
4. Content hierarchy driven by scale contrast (very large vs very small)

**Color Philosophy**: Crisp white (#fafafa) base, deep charcoal (#1a1a2e) for text, muted teal (#2d6a6a) as primary accent, soft sage (#e8ede8) for secondary surfaces. Sophisticated and gender-neutral.

**Layout Paradigm**: Magazine-style editorial grid. Hero uses split-screen (image left, content right). Products in alternating 2-col and 3-col rows. Sticky sidebar for filters on category pages.

**Signature Elements**:
- Overlapping elements (images peeking behind cards)
- Fine-line icons and delicate UI chrome
- Subtle background texture (light linen/paper grain)

**Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth, intentional micro-interactions. Hover reveals additional product info. Scroll-triggered parallax on hero images.

**Animation**: Smooth entrance animations with slight upward drift. Image zoom on hover. Parallax depth on scroll. Cart drawer with backdrop blur.

**Typography System**: Playfair Display for display headings (classic serif with modern flair), Source Sans 3 for body and UI (highly readable). Extreme size contrast — 4rem headings with 0.875rem body.
